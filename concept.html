The Concept

Memory as landscape

Hindsight started from the idea to incorporate two modes of vision in a game. There was no narrative yet. Once we formed our capstone group, we developed a narrative that told the story of a person who had limited vision. Why? They also had amnesia, all because of an accident. Uncovering memories was a concept that we really wanted to explore, which we did through creating Hindsight.

Development

How it was built

Starting in the Fall 2025 semester, we pitched ideas, had several discussions, and created mood boards for how we imagined the game to be. We planned the general storyline, created character personas and designs, and developed essential game mechanics.

Most of the actual development was done in the Spring 2026 semester. Many more game mechanics were implemented, characters were modeled, storyboard layouts were planned and drawn, and sounds were recorded.

Overall, it was a great success. We learned so many new skills, got to see cool projects made by our peers, and ended up with a project that we are proud to have created.
